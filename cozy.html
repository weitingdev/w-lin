COZY​ ​​is​ ​a​ ​mobile​ ​app​lication ​for​ ​office​ ​workers​ ​designed​ ​to​ ​eliminate​ ​employee discomfort and​ ​increase​ ​work​ ​productivity​. Employees are able to adjust temperatures, lighting, and humidity levels to fit their needs. Building managers will be able to analyze this data, and the building's HVAC system will also process these data points to anticipate future trends and issues for maximum efficiency.

This group project involves building a business model, conducting user interviews and surveys, designing an empathy map, journey map, and site map, and making user flows, user stories, and personas of our users. After brainstorming a design direction, collaboration on Marvel App was done both online and in-person. Multiple stages of wireframing were drafted based on user feedback and group evaluations. To conclude, a final presentation was done to appeal to potential investors.
