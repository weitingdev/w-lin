The Genomics and Networks Analysis Lab (GeNA Lab) is a team lead by Dr. Peter Chow-White that analyzes the social impacts of Big Data on the digital world. Their work primarily investigates the risks and opportunities regarding large volume data mining and issues on privacy and security.

The website was built over a span of approx. 6 months, and it is my first web-based school project as lead developer. At this time, I lacked knowledge in best practices, and while it is not exemplary work, it was a valuable learning experience.
