The Terry Fox Humanitarian Award Program (TFHAP) is a scholarship awarded to high school students who are Canadian and permanent residents. Applicants will need to maintain good academic standing and have demonstrated significant involvement in humanitarian activities that provide positive impact on the world or within their community. This government-funded program will help successful applicants fund their post-secondary education through annual installments, paid directly to their institution over a span of 4 years. To learn more about this awards program, please visit the TFHAP Applicant Information section for more info.

The website was built over a span of approx. 2.5 years and it is my second web-based project. As lead designer and developer, I have collaborated alongside a back-end programmer, as well as worked closely with management and a small administration team regularly to bring this project to life.
